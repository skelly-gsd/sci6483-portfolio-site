A Weed Garden (Assignment 00).
Weeds are flowers too, once you get to know them...

Welcome to the garden! It's full of L-System weeds, an algorythm used to model the growth processes of plant development. Toggle the following keys to see what grows, and how:

"-" & "=" -> Change weed type.
"[" + "]" -> Add/remove branching levels.
Left-Arrow/Right-Arrow -> Resize weed (in case it grows too big!).
