VJ Loop
In collaboration with Jimi Adeseun, MArch II 25'
Project Details
Roam arond, or just sit back. This interactive demoscene uses basic shader operations alongside p5 audio capabilities to distort a C-M-Y color cloud to the beat. With music off, the cloud flows peacefully, while mouse interaction brings orbiting cyan and magenta bodies in and out of interaction with the central space.
