I'm Slide Kelly.

I'm an Master in Design Studies & Master in Landscape Architecture Student at the Harvard Graduate School of Design.

A spatial-data-guy-turned-designer, I am interested in anticipatory planning and built environment adaptation to environmental risks. I study fire-adapted landscapes, coastal resilience, and practices of forestry from the urban to the way-out-there. I design landscapes, systems, urban interfaces, and data-driven tools for making decisions about our built environment.
